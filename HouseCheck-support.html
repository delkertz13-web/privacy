Frequently Asked Questions

How does HouseCheck determine which maintenance tasks to show?
HouseCheck uses your home type (single family, condo, or townhouse) and construction year to generate a personalized seasonal maintenance calendar. During setup, you'll answer a few quick questions about your home, and we'll automatically populate Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter task lists based on your home's specific needs. Tasks are pulled from industry best practices and homeowner maintenance guides.
Can I use HouseCheck without creating an account?
Yes! HouseCheck is designed to work completely offline and without requiring an account. Your home data, task history, and contacts are stored securely on your device. You can start using HouseCheck immediately after selecting your home type—no signup, no passwords, no cloud accounts needed.
How do location-based reminders work, and why do they use my ZIP code?
HouseCheck uses your ZIP code to identify your USDA hardiness zone and local climate patterns. This helps trigger reminders at the right time for your region—for example, pipe winterization happens earlier in colder climates and later in warmer ones. Your ZIP code is never sent to our servers; it's processed entirely on your device to determine the appropriate maintenance timing for your location.
What does the Pro plan unlock, and is it worth it?
HouseCheck Pro ($2.99/year) unlocks three premium features: (1) Create unlimited custom maintenance tasks beyond our standard 20 seasonal tasks, (2) Unlimited task history storage and photo attachments, and (3) Export your complete maintenance history as a PDF—perfect for home resale or insurance claims. The free version includes all core features: the seasonal calendar, reminders, and local storage.
How do I restore a previous purchase if I switched devices or reinstalled the app?
Tap the "Restore Purchases" button in Settings to recover your Pro plan. HouseCheck uses Apple's App Store to verify your purchase history. If you purchased Pro on the Apple ID you're currently signed into, restoring purchases will instantly reactivate all Pro features. Make sure you're using the same Apple ID on your new device or after reinstalling.
