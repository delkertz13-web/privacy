How is my financial data protected?

DebtMap works completely offline with zero data sharing. Your financial information never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy and security. No accounts, no cloud storage, no tracking.

What's the difference between Debt Snowball and Avalanche?

Debt Snowball focuses on paying off smallest debts first for psychological motivation, while Debt Avalanche targets highest interest rates first to save money mathematically. DebtMap supports both strategies so you can choose what works best for your personality and situation.

Can DebtMap handle complex debt situations?

Absolutely. Whether you have 2 debts or 10, student loans mixed with credit cards, or varying interest rates and payment schedules, DebtMap handles all the complex calculations. No financial expertise required — just honest numbers and commitment to your plan.
