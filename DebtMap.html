What's the difference between Debt Snowball and Debt Avalanche?
Debt Snowball focuses on paying off your smallest debts first, providing psychological wins to keep you motivated. Debt Avalanche targets your highest interest rate debts first, saving you the most money over time. DebtMap shows you both strategies so you can choose what works best for your personality and situation.
Is my financial data safe and private?
Absolutely. DebtMap works completely offline with zero data sharing. Your debt information never leaves your iPhone - no cloud storage, no analytics, no tracking.
