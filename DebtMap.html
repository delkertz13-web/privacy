Privacy Policy - DebtMap

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

DebtMap ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

2. No Data Collection

DebtMap does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data or information from our users. We have implemented a privacy-first approach where:

  • No user data is collected or tracked
  • No analytics or crash reporting data is sent to external servers
  • No user identification information is gathered
  • No usage statistics are recorded
  • No third-party tracking or advertising libraries are used

3. Local Data Storage

All data created within DebtMap is stored exclusively on your device in the application's local storage. This includes:

  • Debt information you input
  • Payment history and records
  • Budgeting notes and customizations
  • App preferences and settings
Important: All data remains entirely under your control and is never transmitted to our servers, third-party services, or any other external sources.

4. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, security is the sole responsibility of your device's operating system and file system encryption. We recommend:

  • Using a secure passcode or biometric authentication on your device
  • Keeping your iOS device updated with the latest security patches
  • Regularly backing up your device if you wish to preserve your data

5. Permissions

DebtMap only requests permissions necessary for core functionality and only when needed. Any permissions requested are used solely for their intended purpose on your device.

6. Third-Party Services

DebtMap does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. We do not share data with any external parties because no data collection occurs.

7. Children's Privacy

DebtMap does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we do not collect any data at all, there are no special protections needed regarding children's information.

8. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. To delete all data stored by DebtMap:

  • Delete individual entries within the app, or
  • Uninstall the application from your device

Once deleted, data cannot be recovered unless you have created manual backups.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of DebtMap following the posting of revised Privacy Policy means you accept the changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or DebtMap's privacy practices, please contact us through the App Store support channels or visit our website.

11. Compliance

DebtMap complies with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including GDPR, CCPA, and other regional privacy standards. Since no personal data is collected, storage, processed, or transmitted, DebtMap maintains the highest standard of privacy protection.

Summary: DebtMap prioritizes your privacy by design. No data leaves your device. What you create stays with you.
