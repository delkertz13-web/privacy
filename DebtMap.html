Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Debt Snowball and Debt Avalanche? +
Debt Snowball focuses on paying off your smallest debts first, creating psychological wins and momentum. Debt Avalanche targets your highest interest rate debts first, saving you more money over time. DebtMap calculates both strategies and shows you the exact difference in time and money saved, helping you choose what works best for your personality and situation.
Is my financial data safe and private? +
Absolutely. DebtMap works completely offline with zero data sharing. Your financial information never leaves your device - no cloud storage, no analytics tracking, no data collection. We believe your debt information is deeply personal and should stay that way.
Can DebtMap handle complex debt situations? +
Yes! Whether you have 2 debts or 10, DebtMap handles credit cards, student loans, car payments, personal loans, and more. Input current balances, interest rates, and minimum payments, and our intelligent calculator creates your personalized payoff plan instantly. Perfect for recent graduates, families consolidating debts, or anyone feeling overwhelmed by multiple payments.
