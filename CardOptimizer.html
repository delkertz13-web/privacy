Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to CardOptimizer ("App," "we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our App. This Privacy Policy explains our privacy practices, including what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

CardOptimizer is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All data created within the App remains entirely on your device.

2.1 Locally Stored Data

The following information is stored exclusively on your iOS device using local storage:

  • Credit card information (card numbers, names, expiration dates, CVV)
  • Transaction history and purchases
  • Card categories and custom preferences
  • App settings and user preferences
  • Analytics data for your personal use

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it through the App's export feature, which is entirely under your control.

2.2 App Store Data

CardOptimizer uses StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases (Premium subscription). Apple, Inc. handles all payment processing and transaction management. Your purchase data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy at https://www.apple.com/privacy/. CardOptimizer does not have access to your payment information or Apple ID details.

2.3 Automatic Technical Data

CardOptimizer does not collect any automatic technical data such as IP addresses, device identifiers, crash reports, or usage analytics that would be transmitted to our servers. All diagnostics remain local to your device.

3. How We Use Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device, CardOptimizer uses this information solely for:

  • Displaying your credit cards and transaction history in the App
  • Calculating rewards and analytics based on your spending
  • Providing personalized recommendations (calculated locally on your device)
  • Enabling the scanner and categorization features
  • Maintaining your App settings and preferences

4. Data Sharing

CardOptimizer does not share, sell, or disclose any of your personal data to third parties. Your credit card information and transaction history remain entirely within your control on your device.

The only exception is Apple's handling of purchase transactions through StoreKit 2, which is governed by Apple's terms and privacy policies.

5. Data Security

Your data security is our priority. CardOptimizer implements the following security measures:

  • Local Encryption: Data is stored using iOS's native secure storage mechanisms
  • No Network Transmission: Your sensitive data is never sent over the internet
  • Device Protection: Your iOS device's built-in security (Face ID, Touch ID, passcode) protects access to the App
  • No Backend Servers: CardOptimizer does not maintain servers that could be compromised
Important: Since all data is stored locally, the security of your information depends on your iOS device's security. We recommend keeping your device updated with the latest iOS version.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You have complete control over your data. You can:

  • Delete individual cards and transactions within the App
  • Clear all App data by uninstalling the App or using iOS Settings
  • Export your data (if the feature is available) at any time

When you delete data within the App, it is permanently removed from your device. CardOptimizer maintains no backups on external servers.

7. Children's Privacy

CardOptimizer is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately using the information provided below.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can access all your data stored in the App at any time
  • Right to Delete: You can delete your data at any time
  • Right to Export: You can export your data if the feature is available
  • Right to Data Portability: You own your data and can extract it from the App

9. Third-Party Services

CardOptimizer uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit 2: For processing in-app purchases. Privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

CardOptimizer does not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or data collection services. No third-party trackers are embedded in the App.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of CardOptimizer after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
