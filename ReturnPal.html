ReturnPal Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

ReturnPal ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and your rights when using our iOS application.

2. Data Collection

ReturnPal does not collect any personal data. The App operates entirely on your device and does not transmit, store, or process any information on external servers.

3. Local Processing

All data processed by ReturnPal:

  • Remains exclusively on your device
  • Is never sent to our servers or any third-party services
  • Is never shared with external parties
  • Exists only within the App's local storage

4. No Third-Party Services

ReturnPal does not integrate with third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services. Your usage of the App generates no external data transmissions.

5. Device Permissions

ReturnPal does not request access to your device's sensitive features such as location, contacts, photos, calendar, or other personal data. Any permissions requested by the App are used solely for their intended local functionality.

6. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, security is maintained by your iOS device's built-in security features. We recommend:

  • Keeping your iOS device updated
  • Using a strong device passcode
  • Enabling Face ID or Touch ID

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all data stored by ReturnPal at any time by:

  • Deleting the App from your device
  • Using the App's built-in data deletion features

8. Children's Privacy

ReturnPal does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is designed to be privacy-friendly for users of all ages since no data collection occurs.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this policy and posting the new version within the App.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or ReturnPal's privacy practices, please contact us through the App's support section.

11. Compliance

ReturnPal complies with Apple's App Store guidelines and respects privacy regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws through its local-only data processing approach.
