Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

Introduction

PostureBreak ("App", "we", "us", or "our") operates the PostureBreak mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains our privacy practices and how we handle your information.

Data Collection

PostureBreak does not collect any personal data. Our application operates entirely on your device and does not transmit, store, or process any information on external servers.

All features, settings, and reminders are processed locally on your iOS device. No user data is collected, tracked, or shared with third parties.

Local Storage Only

PostureBreak stores all information locally on your device using iOS's built-in storage mechanisms. This includes:

  • Your posture break preferences and settings
  • Reminder schedules and customizations
  • Any notes or logs you create within the app

This data is only accessible on your device and remains under your control at all times.

No External Communication

PostureBreak does not:

  • Send data to external servers or cloud services
  • Transmit information over the internet
  • Connect to third-party analytics or tracking services
  • Require user registration or account creation
  • Collect device identifiers or usage statistics
  • Share data with advertisers or marketing partners

Permissions

PostureBreak may request standard iOS permissions such as notification access to deliver posture break reminders directly on your device. These permissions are used solely to provide the app's intended functionality and do not involve data collection or transmission.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security is managed by iOS's built-in encryption and protection mechanisms. We recommend:

  • Keeping your iOS device updated with the latest security patches
  • Using device encryption and secure lock methods
  • Backing up your device data regularly through iCloud or iTunes

Third-Party Services

PostureBreak does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not integrate with external APIs or services that could potentially collect your data.

Children's Privacy

PostureBreak is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, our service complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our app or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes through the App Store or within the application. Your continued use of PostureBreak following the posting of revised Privacy Policy means that you accept and agree to the changes.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you maintain complete control over your information. To delete all PostureBreak data:

  • Remove the app from your iOS device, which will automatically delete all associated data
  • Alternatively, go to Settings > General > iPhone Storage > PostureBreak and select "Offload App" or "Delete App"

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the App Store or email support at support@posturebreak.app.

We are committed to your privacy and transparency in our operations.
