Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

Introduction

PostureBreak ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

Data Collection & Privacy

PostureBreak collects NO personal data. We do not collect, store, or transmit any information about you or your device to external servers.

Local-Only Processing

All features of PostureBreak operate entirely on your device:

  • Posture monitoring and tracking happens locally on your iPhone
  • Break reminders and notifications are generated and stored locally
  • All settings and preferences are saved only on your device
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or third-party services
  • No analytics or tracking is performed

Third-Party Services

PostureBreak does not use any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data collection tools. The App is completely self-contained and does not integrate with external services.

Your Information Security

Since PostureBreak operates entirely locally on your device, your information is inherently secure. You maintain complete control over all data on your iPhone. No data leaves your device.

Permissions

PostureBreak may request certain permissions from your iOS device (such as camera or motion sensor access) solely to provide the posture monitoring features. These permissions are used only for their intended purpose and no data is collected or shared.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App and on our website. Your continued use of PostureBreak following such modifications constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App's support section or visit our website.

Compliance

PostureBreak is designed to comply with all applicable privacy regulations, including GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws, through its privacy-by-design approach of collecting no personal data.
