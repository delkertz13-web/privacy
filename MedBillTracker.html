Privacy Policy

MedBillTracker

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

1. Introduction

MedBillTracker ("App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

2. Data Collection

No Data Collection: MedBillTracker does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal information, medical data, or financial information with third parties or external servers.

3. Local Storage

All data you enter into MedBillTracker is stored exclusively on your device locally. This includes:

  • Medical bills and expenses
  • Patient information
  • Billing records and notes
  • Any other information you add to the App

Your data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers, cloud services, or any third party.

4. Device Access

MedBillTracker may request access to device features necessary for its core functionality. Any permissions requested by the App are used solely to provide features you explicitly authorize and are never used for data collection or tracking.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Keeping your iOS device updated with the latest security patches
  • Using a strong device passcode or biometric authentication
  • Regularly backing up your device data

6. Data Deletion

You maintain complete control over your data. You can delete any data from MedBillTracker at any time through the App's interface. If you delete the App, all associated local data will be removed from your device unless you have backed it up separately.

7. Third-Party Services

MedBillTracker does not integrate with, share data with, or use any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services.

8. Children's Privacy

MedBillTracker does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Parents or guardians should supervise children's use of the App.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of MedBillTracker following changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or MedBillTracker's privacy practices, please contact us through the App's support channels or via the contact information provided in the App Store listing.

11. Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant privacy legislation. Since we do not collect personal data, the requirements of these regulations that pertain to data collection do not apply to MedBillTracker.

Summary: Your privacy is paramount. MedBillTracker operates with zero data collection and all information remains exclusively on your device. You have complete control over your data at all times.
