PantryGuard Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to PantryGuard ("we", "us", "our", or "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

Key Point: PantryGuard collects NO data. All information is stored locally on your device only.

1. Data Collection

PantryGuard does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data. The App does not:

  • Collect any information about you or your pantry items
  • Track your usage patterns or behavior
  • Access your contacts, location, or device identifiers
  • Send any data to external servers or third parties
  • Use analytics or crash reporting services
  • Require user accounts or authentication

2. Local Storage Only

All data entered into PantryGuard, including:

  • Pantry inventory lists
  • Product information
  • Expiration dates
  • Personal preferences

is stored exclusively on your iOS device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. Data Security

Your data is protected by iOS's built-in security features. Only you can access your PantryGuard data through your device. We recommend:

  • Using a strong device passcode
  • Keeping your iOS device updated
  • Backing up your device through iCloud or iTunes if desired

4. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data. You can delete all PantryGuard data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual items within the App
  • Using the App's clear/reset function
  • Uninstalling the App from your device

5. Third-Party Services

PantryGuard does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, advertisements networks, or analytics platforms.

6. Children's Privacy

PantryGuard does not knowingly collect information from children. Since no data collection occurs, children can use this App safely with parental guidance.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the PantryGuard App, please contact us through the App Store support page.

Your Privacy, Your Device, Your Data: PantryGuard respects your privacy by design. No data is collected, stored remotely, or shared with anyone. This is your personal pantry manager, completely under your control.
