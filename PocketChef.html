Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 7, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to PocketChef ("we," "us," "our," or "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our App. This Privacy Policy explains our privacy practices for the PocketChef iOS application.

Key Privacy Commitment: PocketChef collects ZERO personal data. All data processing and storage occurs exclusively on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any information about you.

1. Information We Collect

PocketChef does not collect any personal information, usage data, device identifiers, or analytics. The App operates entirely offline on your iOS device without connecting to any remote servers or third-party services for data collection.

What This Means:

  • No personal data collection (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No usage tracking or analytics
  • No location data collection
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs collection
  • No cookie or tracking technology usage
  • No crash reporting that transmits data

2. Local Data Storage

All recipes, notes, preferences, and any other information you create within PocketChef are stored exclusively on your device. This data:

  • Remains on your iPhone or iPad only
  • Is not synced to cloud servers
  • Is not shared with PocketChef or any third parties
  • Is protected by your device's security measures
  • Is deleted if you uninstall the application

3. Third-Party Services

PocketChef does not integrate with any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, social media platforms, or data brokers. The App contains no SDKs or libraries that collect or transmit personal information.

4. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security is primarily dependent on your iOS device's built-in security features. We recommend:

  • Keeping your iOS device updated with the latest security patches
  • Using a strong device passcode or Face ID/Touch ID
  • Enabling iCloud backup if you want additional data protection
  • Regularly backing up your device

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You have complete control over your data in PocketChef. You can delete any recipes, notes, or preferences within the App at any time. When you uninstall PocketChef, all local data associated with the App is removed from your device.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of PocketChef following such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Since PocketChef does not collect, use, or share personal data, you do not need to take any action to control your information. You have the following rights:

  • Transparency: This policy clearly describes our no-collection approach
  • Data Control: You control all data on your device and can delete it anytime
  • No Tracking: You cannot be tracked or profiled by PocketChef

8. GDPR, CCPA, and Other Privacy Laws

Because PocketChef does not collect personal data, most data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.) do not apply to our data collection practices. However, we remain committed to respecting user privacy rights and comply with all applicable laws regarding the App itself.

9. Children's Privacy

PocketChef does not knowingly collect information from children. Since we collect no personal information from any user, we do not have special data protections specific to children, but we also do not collect from anyone under 13. Parents and guardians can use PocketChef with confidence knowing no data is being collected from their children.

10. California Privacy Rights

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), California residents have certain rights. Since PocketChef does not collect personal information, these rights do not apply to our data handling, but we honor all privacy principles outlined in these regulations.
