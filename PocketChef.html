Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to PocketChef ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our application. This Privacy Policy explains our privacy practices and how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection

PocketChef collects NO personal data, usage data, or analytics information.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any information about you or your usage of the application. Your privacy is our highest priority, and we have designed PocketChef to operate completely without data collection.

3. Local Storage

All data created within PocketChef is stored exclusively on your device's local storage. This includes:

  • Recipes and cooking instructions you create or save
  • Ingredients and meal preferences
  • Cooking notes and personal modifications
  • Any other content you add to the application

Your data remains entirely under your control and is never transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

4. No Third-Party Services

PocketChef does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. We do not use tracking technologies, cookies, or similar mechanisms.

5. No Account Creation

PocketChef does not require user accounts, registration, or login. You can use the application immediately without providing any personal information.

6. Security

Since all data remains on your device, the security of your information is primarily dependent on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Keeping your iOS device updated with the latest security patches
  • Using strong device unlock methods (Face ID, passcode, etc.)
  • Backing up your device regularly to protect against data loss

7. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data. To delete all PocketChef data from your device, simply uninstall the application. All locally stored information will be permanently removed.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of PocketChef constitutes your acceptance of any updates.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the application's support channel or visit our website.

10. Children's Privacy

PocketChef is designed to be used by users of all ages. Since we do not collect any data, we do not knowingly collect information from children. Parents and guardians can be confident that PocketChef operates with complete privacy protection.
