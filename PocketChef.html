Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to PocketChef ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). At PocketChef, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app. This Privacy Policy explains our privacy practices and how we handle information related to the PocketChef iOS application.

No Data Collection

PocketChef is designed with your privacy as our primary concern. We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data from our users. This means:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • We do not track your location or device information
  • We do not collect usage analytics or behavioral data
  • We do not use cookies, pixels, or similar tracking technologies
  • We do not collect payment information (the app is free)
  • We do not collect or process health or dietary information

Local Storage Only

All data remains on your device. Any information you create, store, or use within PocketChef (such as recipes, notes, preferences, or settings) is stored exclusively on your iOS device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

No Third-Party Integrations

PocketChef does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. Your app usage data is not shared with any external parties or service providers.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security settings. We recommend:

  • Keeping your iOS device and operating system updated
  • Using a strong device passcode or biometric authentication
  • Backing up your device regularly through iCloud or your preferred backup method

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of PocketChef after any modifications constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

Since PocketChef does not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete from our systems. However, you maintain full control over all data stored on your device and can delete the app at any time to remove all associated data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the App Store support page or reach out directly with your inquiries.

Compliance

PocketChef complies with applicable privacy regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and other regional privacy laws. Because we do not collect personal data, the requirements of these regulations are inherently satisfied.
