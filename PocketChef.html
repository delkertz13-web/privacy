Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 7, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to PocketChef ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use of the PocketChef iOS application.

Core Principle: PocketChef does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data processing occurs locally on your device only.

1. Data Collection

PocketChef does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers
  • Location data
  • Browsing or usage history
  • Photos, videos, or other media files
  • Health or fitness information
  • Payment or billing information
  • Contact information
  • Calendar or reminder data

2. Local Storage

All recipes, cooking notes, favorites, and other data created within PocketChef are stored exclusively on your iOS device using local storage. This includes:

  • App data remains on your device only
  • No synchronization with external servers
  • No backup to cloud services (unless you manually enable iCloud backup through iOS settings)
  • No transmission to third parties

3. Data Deletion

You maintain complete control over your data:

  • All data is deleted when you uninstall the application
  • You can delete individual recipes or data directly within the app
  • PocketChef cannot access or recover deleted data

4. Third-Party Services

PocketChef does not use third-party analytics, tracking services, advertising networks, or external APIs that would collect your data. We do not integrate with:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services (unless you choose to use them)

5. Permissions

PocketChef requests only the minimum permissions necessary for core functionality:

  • Camera: Optional, for photographing recipes or ingredients (data remains on your device)
  • Photos Library: Optional, for importing images (data remains on your device)
  • Microphone: Not required
  • Location: Not required
  • Contacts: Not required

6. Security

Since PocketChef operates entirely on your device with no data transmission:

  • No data is transmitted over the internet from PocketChef
  • Your data is as secure as your iOS device
  • We recommend using iOS security features (Face ID, Touch ID, device passcode)

7. Children's Privacy

PocketChef does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since the app collects no data whatsoever, there are no special protections needed.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the app. Your continued use of PocketChef following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the App Store support channel associated with PocketChef.

Summary: PocketChef is a privacy-first application. We collect zero data, store everything locally on your device, and never transmit information to external servers.
