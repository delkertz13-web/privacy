Privacy Policy for PocketChef

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to PocketChef ("we," "us," "our," or "the App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

Data Collection

PocketChef does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, usage data, or analytics.

The App operates entirely on your device with no server connections for data collection purposes. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device information (IDFA, device ID, IP address, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or app interaction data
  • Location data
  • Health or medical information
  • Payment information
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data

Local Storage

All data stored by PocketChef is processed and stored exclusively on your iOS device. This includes:

  • Recipes you create or save
  • Cooking preferences and settings
  • Bookmarks and favorites
  • Any other user-generated content within the App

Your data remains completely under your control and is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

No Third-Party Services

PocketChef does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or similar tracking tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media SDKs
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Firebase or similar backend services for data collection

User Rights

Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on our servers. If you wish to delete data from the App, simply uninstall PocketChef from your device, which will remove all locally stored data.

Security

Your data is secure because it remains exclusively on your device. However, you are responsible for protecting your device with appropriate passcodes and security measures.

Children's Privacy

PocketChef does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we collect no data whatsoever, this policy applies equally to all users, including minors.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App with an updated date. Your continued use of PocketChef after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the App's privacy practices, please contact us through the App's support channels or visit our website.

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with applicable privacy regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and Apple's privacy guidelines as outlined in the App Store.
