Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to PocketChef ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our application. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices regarding our iOS application.

Data Collection

PocketChef does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Our application operates entirely on your device with no data collection mechanisms. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers
  • Location data
  • Health or dietary information
  • Browsing history or usage analytics
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Any other form of personal or non-personal data

Local Storage

All information processed by PocketChef, including recipes, preferences, and user-generated content, is stored exclusively on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or third parties.

  • Data remains under your complete control
  • Only you can access your app data
  • Deletion of the app removes all associated data

Third-Party Services

PocketChef does not integrate with third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data brokers. We do not use any tracking libraries or SDKs that collect user data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Any changes will be posted in the app or on our website, with the "Last Updated" date revised accordingly.

Your Rights

Since PocketChef does not collect or store any personal data, there are no data rights requests to process. You have complete control over any information stored on your device through your device's native settings and app management tools.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the App Store or visit our website.
