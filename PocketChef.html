Privacy Policy - PocketChef

Effective Date: March 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to PocketChef ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our application. This Privacy Policy explains our privacy practices regarding the PocketChef iOS application.

2. Our Privacy Commitment

At PocketChef, we believe that privacy is a fundamental right. We have designed our application with privacy-first principles, ensuring that your personal information remains completely under your control.

Key Principle: PocketChef does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data. All data processing occurs locally on your device.

3. Data Collection

PocketChef does not collect any data about you, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing or usage history
  • Location data
  • Device information
  • Cooking preferences or recipes viewed
  • Account information
  • Analytics or tracking data

4. Local Data Processing

All data generated within PocketChef, including recipes, notes, and preferences, is stored exclusively on your device. This includes:

  • Recipes you create or modify
  • Cooking timers and notes
  • Personal cooking preferences
  • Bookmarked or saved recipes

No information is transmitted to our servers, third parties, or external services without your explicit action.

5. No Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your information to any third parties. Because PocketChef does not collect data, there is no data to share.

6. No Tracking or Analytics

PocketChef does not implement any tracking, analytics, or monitoring tools. We do not monitor your usage patterns, device behavior, or any other metrics related to your app usage.

7. Internet Connection

PocketChef functions entirely offline. Your app does not require an active internet connection to operate, and no data is transmitted online at any time.

8. Data Security

Since all your data remains on your device, its security depends on your device's security measures, including:

  • Your device's built-in encryption
  • Your device passcode or biometric authentication
  • iOS operating system security features

9. Children's Privacy

PocketChef does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Our application is designed for all ages, but we do not collect data from any users.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" of this policy. Your continued use of PocketChef following the posting of revisions constitutes your acceptance of those changes.

11. Your Rights

Because PocketChef does not collect or store any personal data on our servers, typical data subject rights (such as access, deletion, or portability) do not apply to our services. However, you have complete control over all data on your device and can delete the application at any time, which will remove all locally stored data associated with PocketChef.

12. Permissions Requested

PocketChef may request certain device permissions (such as access to device storage or photos). These permissions are used solely for your convenience and to enhance your experience within the app. No data obtained through these permissions is shared with us or any third party.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the contact information provided on the App Store listing.
