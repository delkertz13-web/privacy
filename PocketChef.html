Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to PocketChef. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

Key Commitment: PocketChef does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data remains on your device.

Data Collection

PocketChef collects NO data.

  • We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • We do not collect usage analytics or app performance data
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies

Local Data Storage

All data created within PocketChef is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Recipes you save are stored locally on your iOS device
  • Meal plans and preferences are stored locally
  • Shopping lists and notes are stored locally
  • All this data is encrypted by iOS's standard security features
  • Only you have access to your data

No Third-Party Sharing

Since PocketChef does not collect any data, we do not share any information with third parties, advertisers, or analytics services.

No Cloud Services

PocketChef operates entirely offline. We do not sync data to cloud servers or other external services. All functionality is contained within your device.

Permissions

PocketChef only requests the minimum necessary permissions:

  • Camera access (optional, for scanning recipes or meal photos)
  • Photo library access (optional, for selecting images)

These permissions are used only on your device and no data is transmitted externally.

Security

Your data security is inherent to the local-first design of PocketChef:

  • Data never leaves your device
  • No network transmission of personal information
  • Protected by your device's built-in security features
  • You maintain full control of your data

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can delete any recipe, meal plan, or note at any time
  • Uninstalling PocketChef removes all associated app data
  • No data persists on any external servers

Children's Privacy

PocketChef does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, parents can be assured their children's privacy is protected while using our app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on the App Store and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of PocketChef following the posting of revised Privacy Policy means that you accept and agree to the changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the App Store support section.

Privacy By Design: PocketChef is built with privacy at its core. No data collection, no cloud storage, no tracking—just a simple, private cooking companion in your pocket.
