Privacy Policy - PocketChef

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

Introduction

PocketChef ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the PocketChef iOS application.

Data Collection

PocketChef collects NO personal data, usage data, or any information from users.

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or cookies
  • Usage analytics or app events
  • Crash reports or diagnostics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Payment information

Local Operation

All data remains local on your device.

PocketChef operates entirely offline on your iOS device. All recipes, preferences, and any information you create within the app are stored exclusively on your device and are never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Third-Party Services

PocketChef does not integrate with or use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data collection services. We have no partnerships that involve the sharing of user information.

Your Data Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • You can delete the app and all associated data at any time
  • You can manage app permissions through iOS Settings
  • Your data is never backed up or synced to cloud services by PocketChef

Security

We rely on iOS's built-in security features to protect data stored on your device. Since we do not transmit any information, there are no data transmission security concerns related to PocketChef.

Children's Privacy

PocketChef does not knowingly collect information from children or anyone. Since we collect no data whatsoever, children can use the app safely without privacy concerns.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any significant changes will be reflected in the app with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of PocketChef constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the App Store listing or visit our support page.

© 2026 PocketChef. All rights reserved. This Privacy Policy is subject to change without notice.
