Privacy Policy for HouseCheck

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

1. Overview

HouseCheck ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

Key Principle: HouseCheck collects NO data. All information remains entirely on your device and is processed locally only.

2. Data Collection

HouseCheck does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Photos, videos, or documents you create within the app
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or sensitive personal data

3. Local Processing

All data you create, import, or interact with in HouseCheck is processed exclusively on your device. This means:

  • All information is stored locally on your iPhone
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • No cloud synchronization occurs without your explicit action
  • No internet connection is required for core functionality
  • You have complete control over your data at all times

4. Third-Party Services

HouseCheck does not integrate with or share data to third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data brokers. Your information is never sold, traded, or shared with any external parties.

5. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, security depends on your device's built-in protections. We recommend:

  • Keeping your iOS device updated with the latest security patches
  • Using a strong device passcode or biometric authentication
  • Regularly backing up your device data through iCloud or your preferred method
  • Being cautious when granting app permissions

6. User Permissions

HouseCheck only requests permissions that are necessary for the app's functionality. Any permissions requested will be clearly indicated to you at the point of use, and you maintain full control to grant or deny them.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can delete data anytime directly within the app
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all associated data from your device
  • No data is retained on external servers

8. Backups and Transfers

If you choose to back up your device data using iCloud or other backup solutions, your HouseCheck data may be included. This is entirely within your control. Consult your backup service's privacy policy for information about how they handle your data.

9. Children's Privacy

HouseCheck is designed for all ages. Since we don't collect any data, no special privacy protections are required. However, parents are encouraged to supervise their children's use of the app.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" above. Your continued use of HouseCheck following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how HouseCheck handles your information, please contact us through the App Store or visit our website for support information.

Your Privacy Matters: At HouseCheck, we believe your data is yours alone. We've designed our app to keep it that way—completely private, completely yours, completely secure on your device.
