Privacy Policy

App Name: SinkFund

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

Introduction

SinkFund ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection when you use our iOS application.

Data Collection and Privacy

SinkFund does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data with third parties. All data in SinkFund is processed locally on your device and stored entirely within the app's local storage.

Local Data Storage

The following information may be stored locally on your device:

  • Sinking fund names and descriptions
  • Fund targets and current balances
  • Contribution history
  • Pay frequency and payday settings
  • User preferences and app settings

This data is stored exclusively on your device using iOS local storage mechanisms and is not transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

No Third-Party Data Sharing

SinkFund does not:

  • Collect or transmit any personal information
  • Share data with third-party analytics providers
  • Use advertising networks or tracking services
  • Perform behavioral tracking or profiling
  • Sell or monetize your data

In-App Purchases

SinkFund offers optional in-app purchases for premium features. In-app purchase transactions are processed securely by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information—all payment processing is handled directly by Apple and subject to their privacy policies.

Device Permissions

SinkFund requests the following permissions:

  • Notification Permission: To send optional payday reminders. You can enable or disable notifications at any time through the app settings or iOS Settings.

These permissions are used solely for their intended functionality on your device and do not enable data collection or transmission.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Keeping your iOS device updated with the latest security patches
  • Using a device passcode or biometric authentication
  • Reviewing app permissions in iOS Settings

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data. Your information will be deleted immediately when you:

  • Uninstall the SinkFund app from your device
  • Clear the app's data through iOS Settings

SinkFund stores no data on remote servers, so no additional deletion steps are required.

Children's Privacy

SinkFund does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under 13, please review this policy with a parent or guardian. If you believe a child's information has been processed, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above and, if necessary, through app notifications.

Compliance

SinkFund is designed to comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including GDPR, CCPA, and Apple's App Store guidelines. Because we do not collect or process personal data, many privacy regulations do not apply; however, we remain committed to maintaining the highest standards of user privacy.
