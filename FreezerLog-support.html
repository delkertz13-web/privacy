❓Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add items to my freezer inventory?
Adding items is simple and fast! Tap the plus (+) button on the home screen, then enter the item name (e.g., "Ground Beef"), quantity (e.g., "2 lbs"), and the freezing date. You can also assign the item to a storage zone like "Top Shelf" or "Chest" to locate it physically. FreezerLog will automatically track when it's getting close to the recommended use-by date.
What's the difference between the free and Pro versions?
The free version allows you to track up to 20 items in a single freezer. FreezerLog Pro ($1.99 one-time purchase) gives you unlimited item tracking and the ability to manage multiple freezers—perfect if you have a kitchen fridge freezer AND a garage chest freezer. Pro also comes with premium features like custom expiration alerts and offline recipe suggestions.
How do I enable expiration alerts?
Go to Settings → Notifications and toggle on "Expiration Alerts". You can customize how many days in advance you want to be notified for each food type (meat, produce, prepared meals, etc.). By default, FreezerLog alerts you when items have 30 days remaining, but you can adjust this based on your preferences and food type recommendations.
Can FreezerLog work offline?
Yes! FreezerLog is designed to work primarily offline. Your freezer inventory is stored locally on your device, so you can add, view, and manage items even without an internet connection. The app syncs any offline changes when you reconnect. Recipe suggestions are also available offline from our built-in local database.
How do I restore a previous purchase?
If you've previously purchased FreezerLog Pro on this Apple ID and need to restore the license on a new device, tap the "Restore Purchase" button in the app settings. This will verify your purchase with the App Store and reactivate Pro features without charging again. Make sure you're using the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

🔧Troubleshooting

App Crashes or Freezes

Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, restart your iPhone and make sure you're running the latest version of FreezerLog from the App Store.

Notifications Not Working

Check that notifications are enabled for FreezerLog in Settings → Notifications. Also verify that FreezerLog has permission to send notifications (Settings → FreezerLog → Notifications).

Data Not Syncing

FreezerLog works locally on your device. If you're on a new device, you may need to re-enter your inventory. Contact support if you need help recovering data.
