Privacy Policy for SymptomDiary

Introduction

SymptomDiary ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

Data Collection and Storage

No Data Collection: SymptomDiary does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers. All data remains entirely on your device.

Local Storage Only: All symptom entries, notes, and personal health information you create within SymptomDiary are stored exclusively on your iOS device's local storage.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not transmit data to our servers or third parties
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not share your health information with anyone
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • We do not require user accounts or login credentials
  • We do not access your contacts, location, or other device information

Data Security

Since all your data remains on your device, its security depends on your iOS device's built-in security features. We recommend:

  • Keeping your iOS device updated with the latest security patches
  • Using a strong device passcode or biometric authentication
  • Regularly backing up your device through iCloud or other backup services
  • Protecting your device from unauthorized access

Backup and iCloud

If you enable iCloud backup on your device, the data stored in SymptomDiary may be included in your iCloud backups. This is governed by Apple's privacy policies and your iCloud settings. You control whether to enable or disable iCloud backup for your device.

Third-Party Services

SymptomDiary does not integrate with any third-party services, advertisers, or analytics providers. The App is completely self-contained.

Children's Privacy

SymptomDiary is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Parents or guardians may use the App to manage their own symptom records.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or on this page. Your continued use of SymptomDiary following any modifications constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App's support channels.

Disclaimer

SymptomDiary is designed for personal record-keeping and informational purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider regarding any health concerns.
